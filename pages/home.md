The E-health Lab at the [Institute of Applied Biosciences (INAB / CERTH)](https://inab.certh.gr/)) is conducting research in the field of Medical Informatics and has gained significant funding via national and European research projects. To this end, drug safety and clinical support systems are two key research domains for the eHealth Lab.
